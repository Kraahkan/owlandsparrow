Beautiful photos that tell your story

 

Whether you’re an individual looking to record a special event or a business seeking to connect with a wider audience, we’re passionate about helping you achieve your individual needs by showcasing the best of who you are. It all starts with you.

 

Whether it’s a personal photoshoot or branding for your business – we believe there is beauty in ordinary moments. In all things art, we aim to show what you are and not what others may expect. To showcase your love, triumph, passion and hard work. 

A people-first approach to marketing

 

To grow and reach an audience, authentic photos are a strong foundation you need to connect with people. Whether you are new business and just getting off the ground or an existing brand looking to attract a larger audience – photography and marketing go hand in hand. 

 

Have a desire to reach people through social media, but don’t have the time? Whatever your needs we are here to support! With a couple headshots and photos of your product in hand, we can even make you a brand new website to showcase what you’re all about.

Our Team

Platonic soul mates Meg + Tara met on the North Shuswap. Quickly they realized the had the same vision for their families and entrepreneurial spirits. It was time to merge their talents and passions. Between raising their kids and a few strong coffee’s, Owl + Sparrow was born.

Meg O

A grateful immigrant to the land of mountains and lakes, Meg found her little piece of paradise in North Shuswap a year ago. Becoming a paid artist was a dream not thought possible. After supporting local companies with their marketing and social media needs she has now found a happy work life balance helped by the missing piece to the business puzzle: Tara.

Tara C

A military brat, Tara has lived across Canada and has always had a passion for people watching and rock music. She got her first camera as a gift in 2018 and completed a handful of classes to learn the craft. She was filled with inspiration for photography from the beginning but was able to finally fulfill the dream in 2021 with the encouragement of Meg. Tara lives in the rural North Shuswap with her husband, 2 sons and dog.
